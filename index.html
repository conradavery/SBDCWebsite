About

The Humane Society Silicon Valley

The Humane Society Silicon Valley is a nonprofit organization with a mission to save lives, keep families together, and change the game for pets and their people. This Fiscal Year HSSV will save lives by rescuing nearly 6,800 animals and helping to find more than 6,100 animals with loving families. HSSV will also be increasing its medical surgery capacity to provide more than 10,000 spay and neuter surgeries. Keeping Families Together means HSSV serves as a safety to those in need in our community. This Fiscal Year we will serve more than 10,000 owned animals with medical care and wellness services while providing over 1.5 million meals to animals in need. Your support during SBDC will make a direct impact saving lives and keeping families whole.

Events

Spray A Sig

Each house will be tasked with finding a Delta Sig around campus and recording themselves or their friends shooting their target. These videos must be sent to the Delta Sigma Phi Instagram account before 6 PM. The house with the most amount of assassinations will be awarded points.

Saul’s Skateboards

This will be a community event where each house will come together and be tasked with painting a skateboard deck. Each house is allowed as many people as they would like to contribute to the painting and they will be given an hour to complete the deck. Check-ins will be held the entire time and points will be awarded at 1 point per person. The top three skateboards will be given a bonus number of points.

Delta Dogs

In partnership with the Humane Society Silicon Valley, we will be hosting an event for the houses to get involved and see firsthand how their donations will benefit. Puppies and Dogs will attend this event at the chapter house and will be open to petting and cuddling for all participants. The admission fee for this event will be $3. Houses can also come to a photo booth to get a photo shoot with a puppy in their laps for only $2. Along with this the team captain of each house and a team of her choosing will be tasked to create a dog treat for the brothers of Delta Sigma Phi using human ingredients provided during the event.. The best-tasting dog treat will win points. Dollars for points will be 1.5x during this event.

Saul’s Best Dance Crew: The Showcase

The Final event, the culminating night, is the time to show why your house is the most talented, the most athletically gifted, and truly deserving of holding the title of Saul’s Best Dance Crew!
